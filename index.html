Keith Buhler

A sociologist by training, I specialize in digital issues and quantitative methods.

I'm currently a PhD student in sociology, working with advisors Emmanuel Didier (CMH, ENS-EHESS-CNRS) and Christophe Prieur (i3, Télécom ParisTech). My PhD focuses on the social history of git, a collaboration tool designed by and for programmers.

A former student (normalien) of the École normale supérieure in Paris, I have a Master's Degree in Interdisciplinarity studies (ENS-EHESS) and in Sociology and Statistics (ENS-EHESS-ENSAE).
